Ganghar Cold Storage Solution

Preserving Agricultural Wealth in Arrah, Bihar

Business Overview

Proposed is a 5 metric ton capacity cold storage facility in Ganghar, Arrah to serve local farmers and traders. The facility will primarily store potatoes, onions, and seasonal fruits/vegetables, reducing post-harvest losses and enabling better price realization.

Local Impact

Directly benefit 200+ small farmers in Ganghar and surrounding areas by providing affordable storage solutions.

Multi-Commodity Storage

Temperature-controlled zones for different produce: potatoes (2-4°C), onions (0-1°C), and fruits/vegetables (varies by type).

Solar Hybrid System

Proposed solar-powered cooling with grid backup to ensure uninterrupted operation despite Bihar's power challenges.
